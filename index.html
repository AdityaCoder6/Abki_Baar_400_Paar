Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most asked questions.

What is STEMIFYY and what does it offer?.

STEMIFYY is a premier STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education organization dedicated to providing comprehensive learning experiences for students from grades 6 to 12. We offer a diverse range of courses, workshops, and programs designed to foster curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills essential for success in today's technology-driven world.

What age groups does STEMIFYY cater to?

STEMIFYY is committed to nurturing young minds across various age groups within the middle and high school spectrum.

What subjects and topics are covered in STEMIFYY's curriculum?

STEMIFYY's curriculum is carefully crafted to cover a wide array of subjects and topics within the realms of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. From foundational concepts to advanced topics.

What sets STEMIFYY apart from other STEM education providers

At STEMIFYY, we distinguish ourselves through our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and student-centered learning. Our team of experienced educators, industry professionals, and curriculum developers collaborate to create engaging, relevant, and future-focused learning experiences

Are STEMifyy programs free?

Yes, all STEMifyy programs are completely free of charge. We believe that everyone should have access to quality STEM education, regardless of their financial circumstances.
